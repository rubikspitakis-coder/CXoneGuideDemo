How can we help you today?

How do I book an appointment?

You can book directly through the Patient Portal or by calling our central booking line. If you need immediate assistance, use the chat icon on this page.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers including SA Health, Medicare, and private health funds. Please check our billing section for a full list.

Can I get my results online?

Yes, all pathology and radiology results are uploaded to the Patient Portal within 24-48 hours of your appointment.

Need to speak with someone?

Our Patient Care Coordinators are available to help with complex inquiries or urgent bookings.
